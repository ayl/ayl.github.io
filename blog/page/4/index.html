How to Survive Medical School… Part One.

When pimped on percentage questions, one must confidently choose one of the following percentages: 1%, 5%, 10%, or 20%. I assure you that almost everything in medicine is one of those percentages. If you need to increase your chances, combine the percentages in ranges: 1-5%, 5-10%, 10-20%. Remember there must be an oddball reason why they are pimping you, and therefore go against your gut instincts.

Today I was pimped on some insanely long description of a percentage question, and I honestly had no clue, but I confidently said “20%” and the answer was 15-20%. It’s amazing how easily people are impressed with your luck. =P

Stinking to High Heaven…

Turns out I learned today why I unintentionally sniffed a vial of chloride gas that I created in my basement when I was in middle school: necrotic tissue smells really, really bad. Two patients came in today with really horrible infected wounds to the wound clinic. The nurses were gagging and the attending was fuming, but I could barely smell it. So they let me clean out the wounds with scissors and tweezers, and strangely enough I kinda liked doing it. My attending was duly impressed.

It just may have saved my grade in surgery.

Movie: Freedom Writers…

I saw Freedom Writers today in valiant defiance at the enormous mountain of things I have to do. The movie was awesome… It definitely brought some memories of high school back. I mean my high school wasn’t that bad, and I was definitely sheltered from most of it (other than the homemade napalm in the parking lot and the flying firecrackers lit inside the building). There weren’t too many turf wars between racial groups, but we did have Wars. There were Wars between the high school gang-bangers that I would hear about, like the Great War of 1996 between my high school and the neighboring one. Ok so maybe my high school was kinda like the movie…

Back to reality… Man I wish I had some help…
