Interesting Instructions

Sep 13th, 2006

During our psych rotation, we have “variety” rotations that are suppose to give us a taste of the world of psychiatry outside the bleached white halls of the psych ward. As I was idly reading through the instructions and expectations of each of these rotations, one of the instructions caught my eye: “Don’t park next to the windows.”

One can only being to spectulate on the images of snipper-rifle-carrying, window-jumping psych patients invoked in the mind of reader from the beign intent of the writer in describing a row of reserved parking spaces.
