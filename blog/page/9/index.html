Slowly Going Insane…

Nov 9th, 2006

I don’t think I can take any more stressors… My laptop has been making this strange hard disk sound that I can’t figure out what’s causing it. For the past few weeks even when no program is touching the disk, periodically it will make this short burst of sound as something accesses the hard disk. I’ve tried everything: turning off all the services on my computer, shutting off the indexing and system restore, turning off the internet, turning off the virtual memory, and even reinstalling windows and trying all of the above again. Nothing seems to work.

I liken every moment that my laptop is turned on to the Chinese water torture. You know that the blissful sound of silence is going to be interrupted by that infernal sound but you don’t know when exactly. All you can do is pull your hair out while you wait for it to come. I really think my sanity is going to break.

On Voting…

Nov 7th, 2006

I believe people should practice civil disobedience by not voting. Politics these days are not about issues that are important but more about issues that can be turned into political platforms through which people may be swayed. It is not healthy as a society to waste money, time, and energy on issues that have no practical bearing with no personal conviction of the representatives of either party. It is entirely questionable what one side truly believes and the other side blasphemies using religion for personal gain. In essence, politics of late has become a festering ground of wasted debates and arguments, much to the woe of the country.
