The Night Before I Submit My ERAS...

I couldn’t really fall asleep so I started browsing online a little and I found some really neat photo viewing software. After some very hackish code, I put got it integrated onto my site. I think I finally found a gallery software that I’m happy with. It solves the problem of too many pictures to wade through but allowing you to focus on the ones that you like. Accolades to cooliris!

Click on “Photos” on the far right side of the homepage for an eye-popping experience.

Works best with Firefox + Cooliris.
