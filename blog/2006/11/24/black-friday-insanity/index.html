Black Friday Insanity...

This morning at 4:00 am my alarm went off. I awoke in the zombie like state that usually happens every morning. I stumbled into the bathroom and started brushing my teeth assuming I had to go to work. Then I remembered… It was that day of the year again when Americans show their true colors and become the mass consumer mob flooding the malls and stores everywhere. They participate, for one day, in shopping like Asians.

The line outside of Best Buy this morning at 4:30 am was easily 300 yards long, stretching all the way from the entrance to the edge of the parking lot near McDonalds. At around 4:55, the line started moving forwards and I was finally able to see the entrance of the store. Within the first fifteen minutes of the store being opened, they sold out on all the laptop deals that they had going. I was afraid I wouldn’t get the monitor that I wanted, but I think I miraculously got one of the last ones.

My Best Buy Black Friday experience lasted a grand total of an hour and fifteen minutes before I got back to my car. After I braved the mall, I arrived at home only to find out that the monitor that I got was defective… Doh.
