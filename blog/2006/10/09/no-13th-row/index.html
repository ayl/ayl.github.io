No 13th Row...

I learned two important things yesterday on my way back from Washington DC. First, never sit in the row before the emergency exit row on the plane because you won’t be able to lean back and there’s nothing you can do if the row before you decides to invade your personal space. Second, there is no such thing as the 13th row. I never noticed until yesterday. Appartently, it has gone the same way as the 13th floor. But wouldn’t the entire plane be unlucky if something happened to the people sitting in the 13th row?
