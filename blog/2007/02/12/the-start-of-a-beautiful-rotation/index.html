The Start of a Beautiful Rotation...

None of the three of us were bright enough to email the course coordinator last week, so we all called each other on Sunday and conspired to show up at 7:00 am today. We got there and the attending who will be grading and teaching us saw us as he walked into his office, and said “What are you doing here?! It’s so early! Go grab some breakfast and come back at 8:00 am.”

How can you disagree with that?
